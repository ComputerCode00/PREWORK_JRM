Origin / Members

Coldplay are an English rock band formed in 1996 at the University College London. At first the band was originally named Starfish but before they started playing their first gig, they changed their name to Coldplay. The band made world fame after the year 2000's which brought them up to rockstars in the mainstream music media, in the band are

  1. Singer, Chris Martin
  2. Guitarist, Johnny Buckland
  3. Bassist, Guy Barryman
  4. Drummer, Will Champion
